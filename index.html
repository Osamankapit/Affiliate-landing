FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to trade myself to earn commission?

No. As a Deriv Partner, you earn commission based on the trading activity of people who sign up through your referral link — you don't need to place any trades yourself.

Is there a cost to become a partner?

Signing up for the Deriv Partner Program is free.

Is $1,000/month guaranteed?

No. That figure is a personal goal, not a promise. Commission depends entirely on the number and trading activity of people you refer, and results vary widely from person to person.

Is trading risky for the people I refer?

Yes. Trading CFDs and derivatives carries a high level of risk and can result in the loss of invested capital. Anyone you refer should understand this before opening an account.
